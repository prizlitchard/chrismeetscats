Where Am I?

This joyous piece of Internet chronicles and catalogs the adventures of Chris as he befriends random cats, of course!

Our journey began on a balmy afternoon in St. Johns, Portland. While walking home from the park, our beloved author discovered that Chris has the uncanny ability to befriend all cats, especially the ratty stray ones you probably shouldn't touch.

This glorious offering we present to the Internet gods is a work in progress, so please bare with us.

Also, may the record show that Chris once met a baby goat.
